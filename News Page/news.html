Local Cat becomes SpaceCat


Fluffy, a three-year-old tabby cat from Musheerabad, has become the first feline to travel to outer space and return safely to Earth. Fluffy was part of a secret experiment conducted by the Banana Institute Space Research Organisation (BISRO), which aimed to test the effects of microgravity and cosmic radiation on living organisms.
